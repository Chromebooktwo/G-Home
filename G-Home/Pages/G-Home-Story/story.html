Our Roles

We started G - Home as a personal project within a group, with Milan (myself) as the Owner, and Arthur as the Co - Owner. Along the way, Maya joined as a Co - Owner and helped improve the website. However, she no longer plays an active role in G - Home and has resigned from Co - Owner and became a Partner. We defined roles within G - Home as follows:

Owner: The highest role, responsible for creating the site. (Milan)

Co - Owners: Similar to the owner but with less authority. (Arthur)

Partners: People who contributed code, links, or other help to the G - Home website. (Maya, etc)

You can actually try becoming a G - Home Partner if you contribute anything like code, links, games, or anything else like that to the G - Home website! If you get accepted, your name or username will be added to the Collabs page and listed with your contribution! Contact us through the Contacts page where you will find a Google Form in which you can try becoming a G - Home Partner.

Why We Transitioned To This New G - Home Website

There were many reasons why we transitioned from the old G - Home website to this new G - Home website. One of the main reasons was that we lost access to publish and update the old G - Home website. The inability to publish and update the old G - Home website left us very limited. Soon after, we explained why we couldn’t edit the old G - Home website anymore at:

https://sites.google.com/view/the-end-of-g-home/home.

The reason why we lost access to publish and update the old G - Home website was because the old G - Home website had too many pages for free plan that was updated with less pages per website, the website builder informed us that we needed to remove one of our website pages. However, similar to removing a page on this new G - Home website, it was a big issue, so we decided not to do it. By that point, we were already considering shutting down the old G - Home website for good due to losing interest in it. However, we have found our interest again. This motivated us to redesign G - Home, we made this website to solve most of our issues with the old G - Home website!

The Final Straw

The final straw came when our old Great Page on Google Sites was taken down falsely. We reported the issue, and within an hour, it was restored. However, this was a wake up call for us, if someone wanted to take down G - Home, they easily could. This motivated us to redesign G - Home.

Why We Are Back

After considering shutting down G - Home for good, we realized that creating a new version of G - Home through coding would solve the problems we experienced with the old G - Home website builder! By building a new site, we gained the ability to edit it freely with multiple people and maintain full control over the content. We believe that we can make G - Home better with this remodel of the old website!

We don't recommend using the old G - Home website anymore. It contains spelling mistakes, grammar issues, and an inconsistent tone. Additionally, there are several problems that we’ve fixed on the new site. The old site will no longer be updated, and even if we wanted to make updates, we would have to delete pages to do so. Instead, we decided to recreate the original G - Home website with this new G - Home website!
