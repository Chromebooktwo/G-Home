Our Roles:

We defined roles within G - Home as follows:

Owner: The highest role, responsible for creating the site. (Milan)

Co - Owners: Similar to the owner but with less authority. (Arthur)

Partners: People who contributed code, links, or other help to the G - Home website. (Maya, etc)

You can actually try becoming a G - Home Partner if you contribute anything like code, links, games, or anything else like that to the G - Home website! If you get accepted, your name or username will be added to this page and listed with your contribution! Contact us through the Contacts page where you will find a Google Form in which you can try becoming a G - Home Partner.
