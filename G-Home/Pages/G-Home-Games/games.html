The Game Drive:

The Game Drive is an old project that we started a long time ago, somewhere in the middle of developing the original G - Home website. The Game Drive includes a variety of games, tools, and time wasting games. Some of these games, tools, and time wasting games are almost duplicates too. Don’t expect these games, tools, and time wasting games to be top notch, they are just small games, tools, and time wasting games to play and use when you are bored. Some of these games, tools, and time wasting games were made by us, while other games, tools, and time wasting games were created by other people.

Some of these games, tools, and/or time wasting games may have issues, such as not saving high scores. Additionally, some of these games, tools, and/or time wasting games might interfere with one another, affecting things like high scores. However, for the most part, these games, tools, and time wasting games work well! Also some of these games, tools, and time wasting games in the Game Drive may have weird or wrong names, we wont be changing them because the Game Drive and everything inside of it is an old and legacy project that we probably won't update anymore. In addition, some of these games, tools, and time wasting games from the Game Drive may not work partially or fully on mobile, but other games, tools and time wasting games from the Game Drive do work on mobile. Also if any of the games, tools, and/or time wasting games don't fit properly on your screen, you can resize them by pressing 'Ctrl' and '+' to zoom in, and/or 'Ctrl' and '-' to zoom out.
