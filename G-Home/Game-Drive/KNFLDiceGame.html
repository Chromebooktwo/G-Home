KNFL Dice Game

How to Play:

Click the "Roll" button to roll 5 dice. The goal is to roll specific combinations:

  • KNFL: 5 consecutive numbers (e.g., 1, 2, 3, 4, 5) - 500 points
  • Lil' Street: 4 consecutive numbers (e.g., 1, 2, 3, 4) - 30 points
  • Big Street: 5 consecutive numbers (e.g., 1, 2, 3, 4, 5) - 40 points
  • Fullhouse: A pair and a three-of-a-kind (e.g., 3, 3, 3, 2, 2) - 25 points
  • Stack: The highest frequency of a number (e.g., three 5s) - points are based on frequency.
