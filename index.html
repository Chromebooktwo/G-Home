Welcome to G - Home

Welcome to G - Home! G - Home, also known as Game Home, is a website that serves as a game list, where you can discover new games to play.

Optimal Experience

We recommend using this site with a keyboard, mouse, and a standard computer screen. Not having these may limit what you can do on the site. For example, we do not recommend using an iPad, tablet, or phone to access this website. While the site may still work, it won't provide the optimal experience.

About G - Home

This website is a redesign of the original G - Home site, which can be found at: https://milannikulin.website3.me/. Feel free to visit and explore the old site, but please note that the original version of G - Home is no longer updated and contains numerous spelling and grammar mistakes, which we hope won't be an issue on this updated version. We switched to this new version of G - Home due to major issues with the original site. We hope you enjoy the new G - Home site!
